Delete Your Account

If you choose to delete your account, all of your activity on POPVOX will be permanently removed:

  • your positions on bills will be deleted. Your support or opposition will no longer be reflected in our maps and pie charts.
  • your personal comments will be deleted. Messages you've sent to congress will no longer appear on the site.
  • any appreciation you've registered for other people's comments will be deleted.

There is no 'undo.' Once your account is deleted, your account information is gone, and cannot be retrieved.

We need to keep some records for a period of time for everyone’s protection. If you wrote your Member of Congress, we keep records of what we’ve sent on your behalf so no one can claim otherwise. Please contact us if you have any questions or concerns.

If you wish to delete your account, click the button below. You will be sent a confirmation email with a link you can click to complete the deletion process.
