Keep your own website content fresh and allow users to take action with POPVOX’s customizable widgets!

Features Overview

  • Comment Stream
  • Write Congress

With POPVOX’s Comment Stream, individuals and organizations can:

  • Stream comments in real-time—filtered by bill number, position (supporting or opposing) and their location.
  • Inform users with fresh content and honest, personal stories written by real individuals using POPVOX.com.
  • Inspire others to take action and write to their Members of Congress.

Start Using This Widget Now...

The “Write Congress” widget embeds POPVOX’s powerful advocacy tool onto your own website. Your customized text will explain why your site visitors should support (or oppose) the bills you care about. By using our widget, your site visitors are empowered to write a more compelling message to their Members of Congress.

And since it’s powered by POPVOX:

  • Constituent information is verified for Congressional offices.
  • Messages from your activists are quantified and aggregated with other messages to show true grassroots power.
  • Activists can be assured that their messages are delivered to Congress, and receive a confirmation when their message is sent.

With a Plus account, the widget integrates into your existing campaign tools.

GET STARTED

The POPVOX widgets are easy to load onto any site that allows you to paste html code and embed iframes, including WordPress. Simply complete the customization form and copy and paste the HTML code onto your own website!

The widgets are available at no charge. Users interested in fully customized widgets to fit the look and design of their own sites are able to do so for a monthly fee. Please contact Rachna Choudhry at rachna@popvox.com for details.

or

Comments submitted through the widget will be sent to Congress and posted anonymously on POPVOX. Users will create a POPVOX account if they don’t already have one. With a Pro account you will have access to the information submitted by users of your widget, and letters to Congress will mention your organization.

You may choose from the bills endorsed or opposed in your legislative agenda which are pending Congressional action.

Facebook

With a service plan, any POPVOX widget can be embedded into a “POPVOX” tab on a Facebook Page. Facebook allows you to include a single POPVOX tab with a single widget on your page. To add a POPVOX tab:

  • Visit the POPVOX Facebook App page.
  • Click Add to My Page in the lower left list of links.
  • Visit your page and click on the new POPVOX tab.
  • When prompted, enter your service account secret password xxxx. Do not share this password.
  • You’ll then return to this page to publish a widget to your Facebook page.
