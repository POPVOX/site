The Team

Marci Harris

Chief Executive Officer

Marci Harris brings three years of Congressional experience as Counsel to Congressman Pete Stark, chairman of the Health Subcommittee of the House Ways and Means Committee, a position at the center of the 2009-2010 health reform debate. Marci obtained a unique view of both the constituent interaction of a representative's legislative office and the legislative process of a committee.

Rachna Choudhry

Chief Marketing Officer

Rachna Choudhry has a dozen years of experience leading advocacy and legislative campaigns, most recently for a nonpartisan organization that promotes policies supporting women and families. She is responsible for the site’s outreach and marketing to advocacy organizations, trade associations, and Congressional staff.

Joshua Tauberer

Chief Technology Officer

Joshua Tauberer built the award-winning website GovTrack.us in 2004, which tracks the U.S. Congress and spurred the national open government data community. He is active on the community and policy end of Congressional transparency and technology and is published in Open Government: Collaboration, Transparency, and Participation in Practice (2010, O’Reilly Media).

Tim O’Reilly

Founding Advisor

Tim O'Reilly is the founder and CEO of O'Reilly Media, Inc., a book publisher and the host of conferences on technology topics, including the Web 2.0 Summit, the Web 2.0 Expo, the Gov 2.0 Summit, and the Gov 2.0 Expo. (Photo by Brian Solis.)

William Donnell

Lead Designtist

William Donnell brings his Designtific Method and experience from leading graphic design company Sodium Halogen.
