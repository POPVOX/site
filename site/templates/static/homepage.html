POPVOX is for

Individuals

POPVOX is different from political discussion sites. It is a place for action — and designed by people who understand Congress to deliver your message in the way Congress needs it. POPVOX provides a real-time metric of public sentiment on specific bills.

Much of the technology that simplifies contacting Congress has made it more difficult for Congress to get a read on what is being said. POPVOX cuts through the noise.

Interested in knowning more? Read on or subscribe for updates:

Let’s Get Started

Individuals

POPVOX gets our message to Congress and makes sure our input counts by maintaining a public tally of public comments on pending bills. POPVOX also connects individuals with the advocacy groups and trade associations that can amplify our voices in the policy-making process.

Let’s Get Started

Organizations

Your top priority is to present your positions to Congress and influence policy-making. POPVOX puts your legislative agenda before Congressional staff, helps you keep on top of pending bills, and demonstrates verified grassroots support in a Congressional district.

Let’s Get Started

Congressional Staff

As a staffer, you analyze enormous quantities of information from constituents, advocacy organizations and experts to advise your boss. Thousands vie for your attention as millions of constituent emails, letters and calls flood your office.

POPVOX provides staffers with the information you need to do your job: real-time bill specific reports with verified summaries of constituent and organizational support — and a searchable listing of advocacy groups by issue area.

Let’s Get Started

The Media

Coming soon, POPVOX will provide newsrooms — from small-town newspapers to political blogs — with Congressional district- and issue-specific analysis and contacts for man-on-the-street reporting.
