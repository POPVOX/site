Your Voice. Verified. Quantified. Amplified

{% endblock %} {% block content %}
POPVOX is an open forum for expressing your opinion in a way that Congress can hear it.

Much of the technology that simplifies contacting Congress has made it more difficult for Congress to get a read on what is being said.

POPVOX is different from political discussion sites. It is a place for action — and was designed by a team who understands Congress.

Let’s Get Started

Individuals

POPVOX gets our message to Congress and makes sure our input counts by maintaining a public tally of comments on pending bills before Congress.

POPVOX also connects individuals with the advocacy groups and trade associations that can amplify our voices in the policy-making process.

Let’s Get Started

Organizations

Your top priority is to present your positions to Congress and influence policy-making. POPVOX puts your legislative agenda before Congressional staff, helps you keep on top of pending bills, and demonstrates verified grassroots support in a Congressional district.

Let’s Get Started

Congressional Staff

As a staffer, you analyze enormous quantities of information to advise your boss. Thousands vie for your attention though constituent letters and calls.

POPVOX provides real-time bill-specific reports with verified summaries of constituent and organizational support — and a searchable listing of advocacy group endorsements by issue area.

Let’s Get Started

The Media

Coming soon, POPVOX will provide newsrooms — from small-town newspapers to political blogs — with Congressional district- and issue-specific analysis and contacts for man-on-the-street reporting.
