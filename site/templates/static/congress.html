Reports on Constituent Sentiment

Get a quick read on public sentiment—of your own constituents and others.

POPVOX provides a public, nonpartisan platform for individuals to weigh in on pending bills and register their support or opposition. Our verification process ensures that each individualʼs comments are assigned to the appropriate Congressional district and restricts them to one comment per bill. You can be confident that comments on POPVOX are from constituents and not astroturf.

About POPVOX

POPVOX bridges the gap between the input the public wants to provide and the information Members of Congress want and need to receive. Designed by people who understand Congress, POPVOX is different from other political sites. It is not a discussion forum. It is a place for action. For more about the site, see About POPVOX or Why It Works.

POPVOX is a nonpartisan, transparent, public platform for activity on bills pending before Congress.
